Elimu ya Ulinzi na Usalama wa Mtoto

Jamii ina jukumu kubwa la kulinda watoto dhidi ya ukatili, kupotea na matumizi mabaya.

Sheria ya Mtoto (Tafsiri)

Kila mamlaka ya serikali za mitaa inalazimika kutoa msaada kwa mtoto aliyepotea, aliyeachwa au anayehitaji hifadhi. Pia kushirikiana na polisi kumtafuta mzazi au mlezi wake.

English Translation

Local authorities must provide assistance and shelter to any child who is lost, abandoned, or in need of protection, and cooperate with police to locate parents or guardians.
